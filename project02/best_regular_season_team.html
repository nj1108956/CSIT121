Golden State Warriors' 73-9 Record

Relive the excitement of the Golden State Warriors' remarkable 73-9 record-setting regular season. Dive into the details of this historic achievement, the key players, memorable moments, and the impact it had on the NBA landscape.

Historic Season (2015-2016)

The 2015-2016 NBA season marked an extraordinary chapter in basketball history. The Golden State Warriors, led by coach Steve Kerr and a roster featuring stars like Stephen Curry, Klay Thompson, and Draymond Green, achieved an unprecedented 73-9 regular-season record.

Key Players and Contributions

The success of the Warriors during this season was fueled by exceptional individual performances. Stephen Curry's incredible three-point shooting, Klay Thompson's scoring abilities, and Draymond Green's all-around contributions were instrumental in the team's dominance.

Memorable Moments

The season was filled with unforgettable moments, including buzzer-beaters, record-breaking performances, and thrilling victories. Fans around the world were captivated by the Warriors' style of play and their pursuit of basketball greatness.

Impact on the NBA Landscape

The Warriors' 73-9 record not only solidified their place in NBA history but also sparked debates about the greatest teams of all time. The season's impact extended beyond the court, influencing strategies, player evaluations, and the way teams approached the game.
